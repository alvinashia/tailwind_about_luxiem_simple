About Luxiem

Luxiem is a VTuber unit under the English branch of the Nijisanji agency. They debuted in 2021 December and have already amassed quite a following. The members of the VTuber group Luxiem are Ike Eveland (アイク・イーヴランド), Mysta Rias (ミスタ・リアス), Vox Akuma (ヴォックス・アクマ), Luca Kaneshiro (ルカ・カネシロ) and Shu Yamino (闇ノシュウ) who will be debuting simultaneously. NIJISANJI EN

NIJISANJI project is a VTuber group featuring a variety of talented influencers who create exciting content. The objective of this project is to accelerate the beginning of the next generation of entertainment through various activities and services such as events, goods and digital content, original music, etc.
